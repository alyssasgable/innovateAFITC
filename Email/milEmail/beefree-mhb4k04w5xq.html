AFITC'S FIRST EVER HACKATHON

 
 

Trade in your time and sleep to create something truly unique in an environment optimized for your ingenuity. At the end of the event, participants will present their products to fellow hackers and a panel of judges serving in key leadership positions.

 

In the cyberspace realm of the USAF mission, professional development often requires the exploration of opportunities external to the organization. Such exploration and experimentation with varying technologies employed by industry is limited within a closed cyberspace environment. In contrast to the normal mission environment, this event will give you the opportunity to bring innovative ideas, develop those ideas using those technologies with a community of professionals, and present those ideas to key leaders!
