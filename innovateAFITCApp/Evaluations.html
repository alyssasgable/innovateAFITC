Submission Requirements

Product Read-Ahead

Page Format

  • 8.5 x 11 sized pages
  • Single spaced
  • 12pt Font
  • 1 Inch Margins
  • 3 Pages Maximum
  • Sections subdivided by the subject requirements outlined in the next section:

Subject Requirements

  1. Team Information
    1. Name & Organization of all team members
    2. Describe the problem your team addressed and AF mission area it falls into
  2. Solution/Innovation
    1. Describe of your team's proposed solution
    2. Describe of how the solution will be implemented in the Air Force
    3. Describe your technology and platform choices
    4. Describe any cyber challenges you had to overcome
    5. State if the solution is already on the market and if so, how your solution is different/better
  3. Software Development
    1. Describe the software architecture/function overview
    2. Describe any software that will be developed by your team
  4. Cyber Security
    1. Describe whether the system will handle sensitive information (e.g. HIPPA, PCI-DSS, PII, Classified, FOUO)
    2. Describe how system data will be stored & secured
    3. Describe your information/system/infrastructure security and assurance plan(s)
  5. Infrastructure
    1. Describe where the application(s) & data for the solution will reside (e.g. cloud, distributed, etc.)
    2. Describe the platform and services infrastructure design
    3. Describe the overall reliability, maintainability, & availability

Presentation

Format

  • File Type - Microsoft PowerPoint
  • No Limit on number of Slides
  • Presentation Time Limit - 10 minutes
