What is innovateAFITC?

Held in conjunction with AFITC, innovateAFITC is a cyber challenge where members of government, industry, Cyber Patriots and students collaborate on a project to address some of the national problems we face today. Diverse, multi-skilled teams will compete by presenting solutions to Air Force and local government, senior leadership and key stakeholders. This competition will enable IT professionals from all IT backgrounds such as network communications, cyber security, software development, design, and STEM to quickly collaborate, form bonds of Wingmanship, and share skills and experience. By tapping into the expertise across functional and organizational lines, residents of the community and the Air Force can leverage talent right in their backyard, bringing the nationâ€™s cyberpower capabilities to new heights through collaborative innovation!

Eligibility

Eligibility Requirements

  • 4 min, 6 max participants per team
  • At least 1 government (civil service/military), 1 industry, and 1 student per team
  • FREE registration for government & students, $50 registration fee for industry
  • Individual registrants not already on a team will be matched with a participating team based on team needs
  • Participate in any capacity you desire, bring a positive attitude, and have fun!

Ensure you diversify your team with members possessing expertise in different realms of IT (software, cybersecurity, infrastructure, etc.) to increase your chances of winning the event.

Additional Information

This event will explore your creativity and test your speed. Teams will have 24 hours to submit a read-ahead and present their solution to fellow hackers and a panel of judges serving in key leadership positions.

Meeting space and internet bandwidth will be available at the Renaissance Montgomery Hotel & Spa at the Convention Center as well as businesses in surrounding area will be available for participants to collaborate and work on their projects. Coaches and mentors will also be available to assist teams in progressing towards completion of their projects and to answer any questions and concerns they have.
